# Navy (navy)

The U.S. Navy plays a crucial role in defending national security and protecting the country's interests at sea. It operates a fleet of ships and submarines, as well as aircraft and personnel, to conduct a range of missions, including maritime patrol, reconnaissance, and combat operations. The Navy also performs humanitarian missions, such as disaster relief and search and rescue efforts.
